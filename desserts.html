Fruit flavored popsicles:

Hot take, but I really think popsicles are better than ice cream. Ice cream is way too filling and ngl, popsicle flavours (especialy fruit ones) are much more strong and I don't like the creamy flavor of ice cream. Anyways, here is my recipe for 6 pops:


Equipment:

  • Blender

  • Popsicle mold and sticks

  • Measuring cups or scale

  • Small bowls

Ingredients:

  • 170g(3/4 cups) of fruit juice/smoothie

  • 500g(2 1/2 cups) of fruit

  • 1 lemon

Intructions:

  1. Cut the lemon in half and squeeze the juice out into a small bowl

  2. Add all ingredients into the blender and blend until smooth( about a minute)

  3. Pour the mixture into 6 popsicle molds then place some popsicle sticks in the middle of it

  4. Secure the lid of the popsicle mold or cover it with foil

  5. Freeze it overnight or around 9 hours in the freezer

  6. Take the popsicles out in the mold and enjoy! üòãüç¶üçßüìç
